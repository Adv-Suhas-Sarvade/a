About Me

Advocate Suhas Baburao Sarvade, B.COM, LL.B., LL.M.

I am a dedicated legal professional with a strong foundation in Maharashtra's legal landscape. After gaining valuable experience at a reputed law firm in Pune, I have established my independent practice to provide personalized legal services to clients across Maharashtra.

As a native of Maharashtra, I understand the local legal nuances and bring this insight to every case I handle. My practice is built on the core values of integrity, diligence, and client-centered advocacy.

I am enrolled with the Bar Council of Maharashtra and Goa and am authorized to practice in all courts across Maharashtra. My legal education from Modern Law College, Pune has equipped me with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate complex legal challenges effectively.
